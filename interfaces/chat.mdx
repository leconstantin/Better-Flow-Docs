## Overview

The Chat feature keeps your newsroom connected with seamless real-time messaging built directly into Better Flow. Communicate with team members, coordinate on articles, and maintain workflow continuity without switching between different platforms or applications.

## Key Features

- **Integrated Team Communication** - Chat directly with colleagues without leaving your workflow, keeping all editorial discussions and decisions in one centralized location.

- **Real-Time Messaging** - Messages appear instantly without page refreshes, ensuring smooth conversations that don't interrupt your creative process or editorial flow.

- **User Search Functionality** - Quickly find and start conversations with specific team members using the built-in search bar for efficient communication management.

- **Conversation History** - Access previous conversations with timestamps and message history, making it easy to reference past discussions about articles and editorial decisions.

- **Active User Indicators** - See who's currently online and available with colorful profile avatars and status indicators, helping you know when to expect quick responses.

- **Contextual Workflow Integration** - Discuss article reviews, approvals, and feedback directly within the platform where your content lives, streamlining editorial collaboration.

- **Multi-Device Synchronization** - Your conversations stay synchronized across all devices, so you can continue discussions whether you're on desktop, tablet, or mobile.

- **Instant Notifications** - Stay informed about new messages and important communications with real-time alerts that keep your team coordinated and responsive.

- **Emoji Support** - Add personality and clarity to your messages with emoji reactions and expressions, making team communication more engaging and expressive.
