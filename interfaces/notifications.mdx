## Overview

The Notifications system in Better Flow keeps you connected to your newsroom's pulse with real-time alerts for messages, article updates, and team activities. Access your notifications instantly through the dedicated sidebar panel without interrupting your current workflow.

## Key Features

- **Dual-Tab Organization** - Switch seamlessly between "Inbox" for direct messages and communications, and "Activity" for workflow updates and system notifications.

- **Real-Time Message Alerts** - Receive instant notifications for new messages from team members with sender identification, timestamps, and unread message indicators.

- **Non-Intrusive Design** - Access notifications through a slide-out panel that doesn't disrupt your current work, allowing you to stay focused while staying informed.

- **Contextual Timestamps** - See exactly when notifications arrived with precise timing (12:01 PM format), helping you prioritize responses based on urgency and recency.

- **User Identification** - Quickly identify who sent messages with colorful profile avatars and clear sender names, making it easy to prioritize communications from specific team members.

- **Unread Message Counters** - Visual badges show the number of unread messages, ensuring you never miss important communications from colleagues or supervisors.

- **Activity Integration** - Monitor workflow changes, article status updates, and team actions directly in your notifications without switching between different sections.

- **Persistent Access** - Notifications remain accessible from any page within Better Flow, providing consistent communication visibility throughout your editorial workflow.

- **Smart Filtering** - Separate personal messages from system activities, allowing you to focus on the type of information most relevant to your current tasks and priorities.
