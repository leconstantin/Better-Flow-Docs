## Overview

The Analytics dashboard provides powerful insights into your newsroom's productivity and workflow performance. Get a clear view of article statuses, track publishing trends over time, and identify opportunities to optimize your editorial process with data-driven decisions.

## Key Features

- **Workflow Status Overview** - Monitor your editorial pipeline with real-time counts of articles in each stage: Draft (author-only), Staged (awaiting admin review), Approved (visible to staff), and Deleted (marked for removal).

- **Article Status Cards** - Get instant visibility into your content distribution with clearly labeled status categories, helping you identify bottlenecks and workflow imbalances at a glance.

- **Historical Performance Tracking** - View article analytics over customizable time periods (Last 3 months default) to understand publishing patterns and team productivity trends.

- **Interactive Timeline Chart** - Track article progression through your workflow with a visual timeline that shows how content moves from draft to publication over time.

- **Status Legend Breakdown** - See detailed breakdowns of each article status with color-coded indicators: Draft, Staged, Approved, Published, and Deleted for comprehensive workflow analysis.

- **Time-Based Filtering** - Adjust your analytics view with flexible date ranges to focus on specific periods, helping you assess seasonal trends and campaign performance.

- **Publishing Pipeline Insights** - Understand your content velocity by tracking how long articles spend in each workflow stage, enabling process optimization and deadline planning.

- **Team Performance Visibility** - Monitor overall newsroom productivity with aggregate metrics that help editors and administrators make informed staffing and workflow decisions.

- **Export and Reporting** - Generate reports and insights that can be shared with stakeholders to demonstrate team performance and justify editorial resource allocation.
