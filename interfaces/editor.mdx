## Overview

The Editor is Better Flow's powerful, Notion-like writing environment designed for journalists and content creators. It provides a clean, distraction-free interface where you can craft compelling articles while collaborating with your team in real-time.

## Key Features

- **Notion-Style Writing** - Experience intuitive block-based editing with rich formatting options, making article creation feel natural and efficient for writers of all skill levels.

- **Real-Time Collaboration** - Work simultaneously with editors and team members, seeing changes instantly without page reloads or version conflicts disrupting your creative flow.

- **Auto-Save Functionality** - Never lose your work with automatic saving that preserves every keystroke, ensuring your articles are always protected and up-to-date.

- **Word & Character Tracking** - Monitor your progress with live word and character counts displayed in the bottom corner, helping you meet editorial requirements and deadlines.

- **Article Metadata Management** - Add optional descriptions, categorize content, and manage article properties directly within the editor interface for streamlined workflow organization.

- **Undo/Redo Controls** - Easily revert or restore changes with intuitive undo and redo buttons, giving you confidence to experiment with your content structure and style.

- **Seamless Navigation** - Switch between editing and overview modes effortlessly with the integrated navigation system, maintaining context throughout your writing process.

- **Save & Stage Process** - Complete your articles with the prominent Save button, then move them through your newsroom's approval workflow from draft to publication-ready status.
