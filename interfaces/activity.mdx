## Overview

The Activity section serves as your newsroom's central communication hub, providing a comprehensive timeline of all actions taken by team members. Stay informed about article updates, status changes, and workflow progress without missing critical developments in your publishing pipeline.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Activity Stream** - Monitor all team actions as they happen, from article creation and updates to status changes and approvals, ensuring complete visibility across your newsroom.

- **Advanced Filtering Options** - Use the sidebar filters to view specific activity types: All, Created, Updated, Added to Favorite, Removed from Favorite, Deleted, Restored, and Staged articles.

- **Workflow Status Tracking** - See exactly when articles move through your publishing pipeline with clear status indicators like "approved," "rejected," "staged," and "updated" for each piece of content.

- **User Attribution** - Each activity entry shows which team member performed the action with their profile picture and timestamp, maintaining accountability and transparency in your workflow.

- **Article Progress History** - Track the complete lifecycle of each article from initial creation through final publication, helping you identify bottlenecks and optimize your editorial process.

- **Content Management Actions** - Monitor when articles are added to favorites, restored from deletion, or moved between different workflow stages, keeping your team synchronized on content priorities.

- **Time-Based Organization** - All activities are chronologically organized with clear timestamps, making it easy to understand the sequence of events and recent team productivity.

- **Instant Updates** - Activity feed refreshes automatically without page reloads, ensuring you always see the most current information about your team's work and article progress.
