## Key Features

### Real-Time Messaging

- **Instant Communication** - Chat with anyone on your team with messages that appear instantly without reloading pages, keeping your workflow smooth and uninterrupted.

- **Live Updates** - Experience true real-time collaboration where messages, notifications, and status updates sync automatically across all devices and team members.

- **Cross-Device Synchronization** - Continue conversations seamlessly whether you're on desktop, tablet, or mobile - your chat history and active conversations stay perfectly synchronized.

### Workflow Integration

- **Contextual Conversations** - Chat directly within article drafts, reviews, and publishing workflows, allowing discussions to happen exactly where the work is being done.

- **Team Coordination** - Coordinate with authors, editors, and team members in real-time during the drafting, editing, and approval processes for smoother collaboration.

- **Status Awareness** - See when team members are online, typing, or available, helping you understand the best times to collaborate and communicate effectively.

### Enterprise-Grade Security

- **End-to-End Encryption** - All chat messages are fully encrypted in transit and at rest, ensuring your newsroom conversations remain completely private and secure.

- **Privacy-First Design** - Chat data follows Better Flow's core privacy principles - your conversations stay within your organization and are never sold or shared with third parties.

- **Secure Infrastructure** - Built on the same enterprise-grade security standards that protect all Better Flow features, with SSL encryption and secure data handling protocols.
